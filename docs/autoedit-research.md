# AutoEdit AI Deep Research & Intelligence Report

## What AutoEdit Is
AutoEdit is an AI-powered Adobe Premiere Pro plugin that automates music video editing. It was built by Devin Huynh (Eleven Percent) — a former LA music video director. It handles repetitive editing tasks: beat sync, B-roll trimming, VFX application, and timeline building.

## Core Features
- **Auto Beat Detection & Cut**: AI detects BPM and cuts footage to the beat in 3 styles (Staircase, Manual, Random)
- **AI B-Roll Trimmer**: Removes shaky/unusable parts from beginning and end of clips
- **Auto Timeline Builder**: Builds a structured rough cut automatically
- **VFX & Transitions Applier**: Camera shakes, CRT overlays, film burns, transitions
- **Camera Shake Applier**: One-click camera shake effects
- **Title Card Applier**: MOGRT title cards via Graphics panel
- **BPM Detection**: AI model analyzes track for timing
- **Performance Take Syncing**: Auto-syncs between multiple performance takes

## Tech Specs
- Works ONLY inside Adobe Premiere Pro 2024+
- Requires MP4 or MOV files (NO BRAW, MXF, or RED support)
- Full-length performance takes required (partial takes cause timeline gaps)
- Intel Macs 2018+ only (not guaranteed on older)
- 8GB+ RAM required

## Pain Points from Social Listening (Reddit, YouTube, Trustpilot)
1. **Clip selection is poor**: "It doesn't do the best job at selecting the clips to use even with analyzing"
2. **FX/SFX don't work properly**: Multiple reports of effects not applying correctly
3. **Plugin installation nightmares**: Mac security mode changes, plugin loading cache issues
4. **BPM detection freezes**: Stalls during analysis, requires restart
5. **B-roll selector is primitive**: Only trims beginning/end, doesn't analyze WHAT is in the footage
6. **No context awareness**: AI doesn't understand meaning or context of footage
7. **Limited to Premiere Pro**: No DaVinci Resolve, Final Cut Pro, or web-based support
8. **Format limitations**: Only MP4/MOV, no professional formats
9. **Intel Mac issues**: Older Macs not supported
10. **No free trial for Event Recap**: Can't try before buying
11. **NaN errors**: BPM detection returns NaN on some tracks
12. **B-roll stuck issue**: Large projects stall, need to import in batches of ~20
13. **Plugin cache problems**: Requires holding Alt/Option to reset cache
14. **Only produces rough cuts**: Not a final edit — users still need significant manual work
15. **Limited use cases**: Only music videos and event recaps
